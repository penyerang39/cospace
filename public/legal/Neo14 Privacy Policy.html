2) Scope

  • Service Providers / Sub-processors: Third parties we engage to help deliver the Service (e.g., hosting, support, payments).

  • Public/professional sources (e.g., LinkedIn for B2B verification).

7) Legal bases (EEA/UK/Switzerland)

Where GDPR/UK GDPR applies, we process data on these bases:
